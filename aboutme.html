About Me

Background

-In 2002, after high school, I attended the University of Wisconsin-Oshkosh, there I had aspirations to get a degree in Computer Science as well as Spanish. While I decided to change courses and pursue a entrepreneurial path, I never lost the passion for technology. I also kept up with my spanish skills and love overcoming the language barrier with native speakers. While still living in Oshkosh I found myself in the service industry again, and realized developing customer relations was something I had been missing. For 10 years I was partnered in a bar/restaurant which allowed me to be my own boss and work with a tight group of motivated individuals that quickly became family. While this was a dream job for me I knew it was not my long term goal. My wife, two kids and I decided to move to Arkansas and get away from the extreme colds of Wisconsin winters, while simultaneously pursuing our homesteading goals. In my current position I am working for a large scale manufacturer while repursuing my tech based goals. We have also begun raising chickens for the eggs and pest control with plans to expand into other livestock such as cattle, pigs, goats and a large scale garden for our use as well as the surrounding communities.
